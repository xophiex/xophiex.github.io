A Brief Absence

September 11, 2022

It’s been two weeks. A lot to talk about. But I won’t. I sometimes think that I worry over nothing. That if I stopped worrying then things would get better. But for me that is not possible. I think the future is going to be okay. I have a solid plan B and plan C lined up. But I really really want what’s in front of me. I really do. And I think this time I got a chance.

HRT is actually going smoother than I thought. My skin tone lightened considerably and I now have hips! I didn’t think it would happen, but it did! The fat redistributed itself by week three!. I haven’t had the time to measure it myself because of all the things thats going on now.

I am not quite sure if breast growth has started. It feels really jiggly. But the budding has not started. At this point it’s just fat. I don’t know what I will do once that starts. Probably stop hormones and stay on blocker?
